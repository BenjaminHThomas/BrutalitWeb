Mini Pizzas


This is quite possibly the easiest recipe ever.

Ingredients:

  • One puff Pastry Sheet
  • 170g Tomato Paste
  • 1 Cup of Shredded Cheddar
  • Optional: bacon bits

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan-forced
  2. Cut Pastry into small rounds
  3. Spread half a teaspoon of tomato paste on each round
  4. Sprinkle toppings on pizza rounds
  5. Bake for 10 minutes
